Gandalf: Aries ♈︎

Gandalf is an Aries. Aries represents the beginning of the astrological new year—it begins the new cycle. Gandalf is brave when met with the Balrog in Moria and faces his foe with dignity and power. Although he falls into shadow in Moria, he is reborn into Gandalf the White. Gandalf is not only a natural born leader, but he is also passionate and quite loyal to Bilbo and Frodo.

Sam: Taurus ♉︎

Sam is a Taurus. Tauruses are known for their strength, endurance, and loyalty. They are down-to-earth and practical, but also have a deep love for nature and beauty. They also have a profound keeness for food. Sam has a green thumb and he's always right behind Frodo with a little bit of Lembas bread.

Legolas: Gemini ♊︎

Legolas is a Gemini. Geminis are known for being deeply curious and adventurous. They spend time deep in study, and go out beyond themselves to quench their thirst for new knolwedge. Legolas is an elf and elf folk usually stay out of mortal affairs, however Legolas decides to join the fellowship anyway.

Merry: Cancer ♋︎

Merry is most likely a Cancer zodiac sign. Cancers are known for their emotional depth, intuition, and loyalty. They are often introspective and guarded with their feelings. Merry is a complex character who is fiercely loyal to the fellowship. He risked his life for the fellowship and was always there when they needed him. Cancers have a strong sense of duty and family, and Merry is no exception.

Sauron: Leo ♌︎

Leo is ruled by the sun, the most powerful luminary in our solar system. Leo has many positive qualities, some of which being power, confidence and energy. However, when unchecked, Leo can become a consuming power and can spell bad new for themselves and those around them. Sauron created a mighty power that was capable of helping and creating positive change around him. Sauron chose to use this power for ill, for to serve his own agenda above all else. This relationship to power is why Sauron is a Leo.

Frodo: Virgo ♍︎

Virgos are ruled by Mercury; they are cunning, giving and can be down to earth. They tend to be perfectionists and take on too much and to do so alone. Frodo very selflessly volunteers himself as ring barer, to take this burden to Mordor. On several occasions, Frodo attempts to leave the fellowship behind, to go his path alone in order to spare his friends. Virgos tend to forget that nothing worth doing can't be done with friends.

Galadriel: Libra ♎︎

Libra respresents the ideals of balance and harmony. They are judicious, friendly and compassionate, as well as living lavishly. Galadirel is an elven ruler, and when she is offered the one ring, she takes this offer seriously, but is able to pass the test: she refuses the power she knows can destroy her.

Gimli: Scorpio ♏︎

Scorpio is a deeply feeling and unshakably loyal sign. They are mysterious, very set in their ways, and sensitive. Throughout his time in the fellowship, Gimli comes to love and appreciate his companions for who they are, even when they're different from him. It takes Gimli time to warm up to Legolas, but once they've bonded, they are inseparable and would do anything for each other. Gimli is very much a dwarf and very much a Scorpio.

Pippin: Sagittarius ♐︎

Sagittarius is much like Gemini; they are creative and love adventure. Pippin's journey on the fellowship brings him so far outside of his everyday life, more than he really bargained for. He moves away from being a smaller-minded person and learns some hard lessons after adventuring too close to Saurons flames. Pippin is bright, passionate and exciting, a classic Sagittarius.

Smeagol: Capricorn ♑︎

Capricorn is an earthy, amitious and regimented sign. Capricorns have been known to do anything for power. Smeagol used to be so much like the Hobbits of the shire before he was corrupted by the one ring. He still shows signs of being highly regimented and driven by the beauty and power of the ring.

Boromir: Aquarius ♒︎

Much like Capricorn and Leo, Aquarius also has a fascination with power. They are known as the humanitarians of the zodiac and tend to seek collective power for good. Boromir has been fighting a war throughout his life and watched many of his countrymen die at the hands of the evils rising in Mordor. It makes sense that he saught out the ring to save his people; his intentions were pure and they drove him to madness. Even after failing Frodo, Boromir jumps in to save Pippin and Merry.

Aragorn: Pisces ♓︎

Aragorn is the Dúnedain is the blood of the old kings, however, he ushers in a new age of men after the war of the ring. Since first meeting Frodo, Sam, Merry & Pippin in Bree, his intuition and inner wisdom helped guide the fellowship through tough and dark times. Pisces are feelers, but they also know when to let go of control and allow things to move as they must.
